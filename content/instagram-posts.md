## Visual style notes (from batch 1)

- **Backgrounds:** Concrete/stone textures (gray, warm beige, sand), white plaster, soft window-light photography — all muted, natural, tactile
- **Typography on images:** Courier/typewriter-style monospace serif — consistent across all posts
- **Logo mark:** R&P ampersand monogram, bottom-right corner, white or dark depending on bg
- **Format:** Square (1:1), all consistent
- **Color palette in images:** Essentially monochrome — blacks, grays, warm whites, occasional blue-gray. No vibrant color. This maps perfectly to the light editorial redesign direction.
- **Bilingual content:** At least one Portuguese/English post — worth creating a dedicated tag or noting in post metadata
