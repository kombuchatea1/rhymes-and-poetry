A small one. Read it. Then look up.

<div class="poem-block">

Before you know it

This will be a memory

So live presently

</div>

The future-tense version of every present moment is *I wish I'd noticed.* The poem is just a small request to skip that step.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
