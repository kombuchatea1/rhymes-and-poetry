A piece written in the exact key of the questions that won't let you sleep. Not looking for an answer. Just sitting with the asking.

<div class="poem-block">

Feeling like all time is gone<br>
Bygones go beyond belief<br>
Delicately flowing in the wind<br>
Is death the only true relief?

What if I choose wrong?<br>
What if I choose right?<br>
What if it's for nothing?<br>
Thoughts can keep me up at night

I wonder — can we make a deal with fate?<br>
Can we make our own mistakes?<br>
Can we change our destiny?<br>
Even for the worse, it's for our sake

</div>

The closing line is the part I keep returning to. *Even for the worse, it's for our sake.* If we get to choose, then even the wrong choices are ours, and that's a kind of dignity. The wrong path is still your path. You walked it.

— JTC
