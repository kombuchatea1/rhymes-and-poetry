A haiku. Some questions are worth asking precisely because they don't have answers.

<div class="poem-block">

Do we ever choose?

Or do things simply happen?

We will never know

</div>

The not-knowing isn't a failure. It's the actual condition of being alive. We move forward anyway. That movement *is* the answer, even when the question stays open.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
