A haiku. Some days don't need analysis.

<div class="poem-block">

Lost in the beauty of it all

With not a care in the world

Like river streams, it flows; Good Days

</div>

The good days don't announce themselves. They just happen. The work is mostly noticing them while they're happening, instead of after.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
