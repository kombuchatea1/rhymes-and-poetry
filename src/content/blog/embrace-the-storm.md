This is for the night-version of the question *am I the only one who feels this?* The answer is no. You aren't.

<div class="poem-block">

You're not alone<br>
I know it may feel that way<br>
But you're not<br>
We all go through it

Pain and sadness<br>
Joy and laughter<br>
Love and heartbreak<br>
Take solace in this

Life has many lessons<br>
I'm still learning them<br>
But I do know this: life can give<br>
And life can take

In the blink of an eye<br>
Things can change<br>
For better or for worse<br>
So embrace the storm

Because when you do<br>
You'll allow yourself<br>
To be alive — even if it hurts<br>
But to live… means experiencing it all

</div>

To live means experiencing it all. The pain too. The pain especially. We get told to chase the high parts and avoid the low ones, but the low ones are where most of the depth happens — the ones who've felt it the hardest are usually also the ones who can love the deepest.

Embrace the storm. Not because it won't hurt. Because the alternative is not feeling much of anything, and that's not a life worth protecting.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
