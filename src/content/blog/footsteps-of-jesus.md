Sometimes the shortest piece is the sharpest one. This is for the gap between identification and practice.

<div class="poem-block">

Everyone calls themselves

Christian…or Religious

Yet no one got the balls

To follow the footsteps of Jesus

</div>

The label is easy. The behavior is hard. The poem isn't aimed at any particular faith — it just notices that for most belief systems, the *acting like it* part is the part most people skip.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
