The paradox of our moment, written down in plain language. We've never had more access to each other and never felt less close. The poem just sits with that.

<div class="poem-block">

We're so divided

Yet interconnected

How can we be so informed

Yet so frustrated?

These thoughts plague my mind

But hopefully these wounds

Will heal with time

</div>

I don't think the technology is the villain. I think the way we use it is the diagnosis. The hope at the end isn't naive — it's just the only kind of hope worth having about something this big. *Hopefully these wounds will heal with time.*

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
