Some loves come into your life and rewrite the calendar. You weren't keeping track of seasons before. Now you are.

<div class="poem-block">

She taught me things

Like Love in Spring

What beautiful days

Lost within her gaze

</div>

The simplest poems are the hardest to argue with. There's no rhetoric here. Just a record of being changed.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
