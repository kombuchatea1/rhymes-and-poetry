The longer I pay attention, the more I notice that life keeps offering the same lessons in slightly different costumes.

<div class="poem-block">

I see trends and themes<br>
Embedded in our everyday<br>
Little lessons from patterns<br>
They test you in every way

Just last week, I felt this sense<br>
Of resistance, of hopelessness<br>
When just the week before<br>
I felt, in my progress, pridefulness

Some days go fast<br>
And other days feel slow<br>
Some days are meant for rest<br>
Others are meant to go, go, go!

Before you know it though<br>
That moment will be a memory<br>
And I try to be conscious<br>
That I embraced it carefully (and carefree)

</div>

The trick isn't avoiding the lows. It's noticing the patterns inside them. Once you can see the loop, you can choose what to do the next time it comes around.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
