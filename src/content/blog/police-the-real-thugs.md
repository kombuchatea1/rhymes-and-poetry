Written in the summer of 2020, when a lot of us were trying to figure out what to say and whether saying anything was enough. I was angry. I still am, on the days I let myself be.

<div class="poem-block">

It's time to police the real thugs

Denial's been America's drug

We cannot rely on government

Only those within our environment

Cuz change was needed yesterday

Black Lives Matter; stand up to the hate

</div>

A lot of the real protective work in any community happens between neighbors, not from the top down. The institutions move slowly when they move at all. The people in your immediate environment — friends, neighbors, colleagues — are usually who actually show up.

I keep this one on the site because it's part of my voice from that moment, and pretending I didn't write it would be its own kind of dishonesty.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
