A haiku, about the only thing that consistently does what nothing else does.

<div class="poem-block">

Writing helps calm me

It brings me a peace of mind

That nothing else can

</div>

I tried therapy. I tried running. I tried meditation. They all help. None of them quite do what writing does. The page doesn't talk back, doesn't judge, doesn't make you wait. The page just receives.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
