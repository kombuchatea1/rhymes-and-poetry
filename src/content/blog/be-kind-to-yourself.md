A small one. Read it slowly.

<div class="poem-block">

Relax and calm down

Ego has no place in peace

Be kind to yourself

</div>

The ego is great at lots of things. Peace isn't one of them. Most days, the kind voice is the one that's actually telling you the truth.

— JTC
