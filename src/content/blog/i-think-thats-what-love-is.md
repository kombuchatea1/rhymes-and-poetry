A small piece. Plain and exact, the way the realization itself was.

<div class="poem-block">

I opened my eyes<br>
And realized that<br>
Your pain is mine<br>
And so is your happiness

When I see you smile<br>
It brightens my day<br>
And when you cry<br>
I'll cry with you

Because we share<br>
The ups and downs<br>
The ebbs and flows<br>
I think that's what love is

</div>

Love rearranges what counts as *mine.* Your pain is now mine. Your joy is now mine. The shared circuit isn't a bug; it's the whole point.
