A small one. Read it twice — once forward, once with the meaning of "now" in mind.

<div class="poem-block">

Presently dreaming

Looking forward to the now

I can hardly wait

</div>

Most of us spend our lives looking forward to a *next moment* that, when it arrives, we won't be looking at because we'll be looking forward to the moment after that. The poem is a small short-circuit. The thing you're looking forward to is the moment you're already in.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
