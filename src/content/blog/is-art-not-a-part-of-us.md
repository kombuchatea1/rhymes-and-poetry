The art that sticks to you isn't decoration. It becomes part of the fiber. This is for that.

<div class="poem-block">

Some stories change lives<br>
A poem can make you cry<br>
Films have inspired<br>
And a portrait can feel alive

Is art, then, not a part of us?<br>
Intertwined with feelings<br>
And our desires, our thoughts<br>
Caught in the fiber of our being

Believing and being inspired<br>
By those who dared to create<br>
To meditate upon what captures us<br>
And share what's deep within our souls

</div>

Whatever moves you is also instructing you. Pay attention to the stuff that lands. It's pointing somewhere.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
