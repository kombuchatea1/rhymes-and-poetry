This is for the version of you that you used to be. The one who's still in there, somewhere.

<div class="poem-block">

Hello old friend<br>
We meet once again<br>
It's been so long<br>
You hardly look the same

I missed you<br>
You're so different now<br>
But there's still some of that you<br>
In there somewhere

I'm glad to see you still smile<br>
From what once brought you joy<br>
Sometimes going back to your roots<br>
Can only help you blossom

I thought you were gone<br>
And that made me sad<br>
But I'm happy you came back<br>
To where it all began

</div>

You don't actually lose the past versions of yourself. They get buried, sometimes, by what came after. But they're still in there. *Hello old friend.* The little ones survive longer than you'd think.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
