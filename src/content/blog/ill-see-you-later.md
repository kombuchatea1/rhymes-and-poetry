Some relationships don't end. They just go on pause indefinitely, and you find a way to be okay with the pause.

<div class="poem-block">

I wish we were close

But we're not — it's okay

I'll see you later.

</div>

*I'll see you later* is what you say when you're not ready for *goodbye* but you also know *soon* isn't true. It's the open door that nobody walks through. It does its job anyway.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
