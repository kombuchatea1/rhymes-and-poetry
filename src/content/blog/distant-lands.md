A haiku. Some peace can only be reached by going far enough away to feel small again.

<div class="poem-block">

Nourishing my soul

By traveling distant lands

This is peace I seek

</div>

Travel doesn't fix you. But the right kind of travel reminds you that you're a small thing in a large world, and that's often the perspective the rest of life keeps trying to take from you.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
