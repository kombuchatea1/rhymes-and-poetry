Most of us want to like ourselves at the end. The poem is just an honest list of the things that requires.

<div class="poem-block">

I hope I can be proud<br>
Of the journey I'm on<br>
Of the choices I make<br>
Of what's come and gone

I hope to make peace with it all<br>
The good and the bad<br>
The heartaches, the growing pains<br>
Embrace being happy, embrace being sad

I want to make the right decisions<br>
But not hurt a soul<br>
Is that even possible?<br>
I'll continue setting goals

I hope you'll be proud<br>
Of all the things you do<br>
Of the lives you'll impact<br>
Of how there's only one you

</div>

The closing flips the camera around. The hope isn't just for me. It's for you too. *There's only one you.* Live like that's true.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
