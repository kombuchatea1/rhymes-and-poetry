A piece that doesn't try to flatten the variety of life. Just sits with it, and ends with the only instruction that fits all of it.

<div class="poem-block">

Whether they're stormy winds<br>
Or calming seas<br>
There are lessons in both<br>
And what they do to me

I look at ups and downs<br>
Highs and lows<br>
They are natural to this journey<br>
Endless ebbs and flows

And there are those who know<br>
Those who don't<br>
There are those who seek wisdom<br>
And those who simply won't

But on this note, I wrote down<br>
The most important lesson of them all<br>
Give yourself the time you need<br>
But don't stay down; stand tall

</div>

*Give yourself the time you need.* That's the permission part. *But don't stay down; stand tall.* That's the rest. Both halves matter; missing either one is how people get stuck.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
