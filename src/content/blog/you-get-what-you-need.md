My dad said this to me at some point and I didn't understand it for a long time. I do now.

<div class="poem-block">

My dad once told me

You do not get what you want

You get what you need

</div>

The thing you wanted was usually for the person you used to be. The thing you got was for the person you were going to become. It feels unfair until it doesn't, and then it feels like a gift.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
