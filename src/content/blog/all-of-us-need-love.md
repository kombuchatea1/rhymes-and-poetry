A list poem. People come in many shapes. The thing under the shapes is mostly the same.

<div class="poem-block">

Some will let it go<br>
Some will let it show<br>
Others will stay silent<br>
Even when they know

Some will sit and cry<br>
While others will deny<br>
Some of us will choose<br>
Even when not knowing why

Some will make a path<br>
While some expect it all<br>
Some will always stay with you<br>
Until they see you fall

Some need to be thought of<br>
Some of us need respect<br>
But all of us need connection<br>
And all of us need love

</div>

You can sort people endlessly by how they grieve, how they show up, how they handle the hard parts. The sorting is interesting. The bottom line isn't sortable: *all of us need connection. All of us need love.* That's it. That's the whole list, finally.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
