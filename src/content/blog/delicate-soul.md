Some people experience the world at a baseline. Others are tuned a little higher — every signal lands harder, every wave feels more like weather. This poem is from one of those.

<div class="poem-block">

When I feel<br>
I feel deeply

When I listen<br>
I absorb every word

When I cry<br>
It's like a downpour

When I'm happy<br>
It's like ecstasy

And when I love<br>
I love with all my heart

So be gentle on this delicate soul<br>
For I know no other way to be

</div>

If you've ever been told you're *too much* — too sensitive, too intense, too easily moved — this is for you. You aren't too much. You're at the right volume for the kind of life you're built for. The work is mostly finding the people who can hear you at full volume without flinching.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
