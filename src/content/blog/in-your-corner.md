Some poems are for everyone. This one is for a specific list of people in my life. They know who they are. If you're reading it and someone you love comes to mind — they probably do too.

<div class="poem-block">

For those who I love<br>
And care for the most<br>
I want you to know<br>
I want what's best for you

Like what brings your heart joy<br>
And what soothes your soul<br>
I hope your dreams come true<br>
Even if it takes some time

Know that I'm in your corner<br>
Even if I'm not around<br>
And if you're sad<br>
Know that I don't want you to be

Know that you have someone<br>
Who's there for you<br>
Even if we haven't talked for awhile<br>
I promise it's going to be okay

</div>

The people we love don't always know it on the days we're not in touch. We assume they know. They assume they know. Sometimes they don't.

This is a small attempt to close the gap. If you have someone you've been meaning to text — this is your sign.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
