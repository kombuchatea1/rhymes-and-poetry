You don't have to wait until January. The work of becoming is the work of every morning.

<div class="poem-block">

A new year is upon us<br>
But every day's a new beginning<br>
A chance to live your dream<br>
To start a life worth living

As we grow and time passes by<br>
What legacy will you leave behind?<br>
If you work hard, success will surely follow<br>
If you're lucky, peace is what you'll find

What if today's the day?<br>
Where you finally begin the journey<br>
Of becoming who you're meant to be<br>
Finally unfolding the depths of your story

It's never too late, until it is<br>
We don't have time to waste<br>
The truth is quite simple<br>
Every day is precious to fate

</div>

The big resets are nice. The small ones are more real. *Every day is precious to fate* — even the boring ones. Especially the boring ones, sometimes.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
