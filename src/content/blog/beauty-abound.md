A haiku. Sometimes the small forms hold the largest reminders.

<div class="poem-block">

Amongst the chaos

There is still beauty abound

Just make sure you look

</div>

The chaos is doing its job — broadcasting itself loudly enough to claim all your attention. The beauty isn't going to fight for it. The beauty is just sitting there, available, waiting for you to remember it's still there.

Look up from the feed. The trees haven't gone anywhere.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
