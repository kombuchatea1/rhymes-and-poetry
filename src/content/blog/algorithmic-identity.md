A harder one to write because the diagnosis applies to me too. We're all in the same machine. The poem is for the part of all of us that knows.

<div class="poem-block">

I see people less friendly<br>
Their rhetoric more deadly<br>
We're slaves to an algorithmic identity<br>
That slowly kills our own humanity

The origin story of an atrocity<br>
Comes from where your bias begins<br>
No one wins from a lack of decency<br>
Clickbait, news fake, educational sins

Chaos runs rampant<br>
When outrage is attachment<br>
And what we know as truth<br>
Is simply beliefs fitting into narratives

</div>

The algorithm rewards outrage because outrage is engagement. We're not the customers; we're the product, and outrage is the most profitable version of us. The first move out is just naming the loop. The second is choosing not to perform inside it.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
