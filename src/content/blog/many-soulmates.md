The word *soulmate* gets sold as singular. One per customer, never available again. This is the version someone offered me when I needed a different shape for it.

<div class="poem-block">

"But… she was my soulmate"

She was the one and now she's gone

She smiled, "you'll have many soulmates"

Some can be a day, others lifelong

</div>

Some soulmates are decades. Some are a single conversation on a flight you'll never repeat. The word doesn't measure the length. It measures the recognition.

If you've lost one — the long-form kind — there are more coming. Not replacements. New ones, doing different work, with their own running time.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
