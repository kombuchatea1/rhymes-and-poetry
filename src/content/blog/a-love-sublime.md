Some love poems take a hundred lines. This one takes four.

<div class="poem-block">

I want to dance with you<br>
'till the end of time

I want to laugh with you<br>
A Love Sublime

</div>

The dancing and the laughing are the whole list. Everything else is a complication that the love itself eventually simplifies.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
