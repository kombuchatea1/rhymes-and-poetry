The best bars sometimes call themselves bad before anyone else can. Owning the bit lets you swing freer.

<div class="poem-block">

Extra Extra! Read all about it!

I'm dishing out symbolic bars

Call me Kurt Vonnegut

Bearded like an animal

Intellect level Hannibal

Always terrible but lyrical

</div>

*Always terrible but lyrical.* That's the contract. You don't have to be good every time. You have to be lyrical every time. The terrible ones are still part of the practice.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
