A thought experiment, four lines long. Whoever shows up first when you read it is who you should probably call this week.

<div class="poem-block">

If the world was ending

How much would you cry?

Would your tears come with laughter?

Or with pain in your eyes?

</div>

The shape of the tear is the shape of the life. Laughter-tears mean you spent your time well. Pain-tears mean there were things you didn't finish saying. Most of us would have some of both, and that's not a failure — it just tells you what's left to do.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
