A list of permissions. A reminder that the contradictions inside us aren't bugs — they're the design.

<div class="poem-block">

You can grow and still grieve<br>
You can feel low and still achieve<br>
You can love and still let go<br>
A goodbye can lead to a better hello

You can be lost and still have faith<br>
You can be skeptical but still embrace<br>
You can be sane and still lose your mind<br>
It comes down to how you use your time

You can be angry and still forgive<br>
You can be kind but never give<br>
You can feel pain but still persevere<br>
I hope it doesn't take long for you to heal

You can have it all and still feel alone<br>
You can be scared but leave your comfort zone<br>
You can change and still have work to do<br>
For we're imperfect, always beginning anew

</div>

The grown-up version of self-knowledge isn't picking a side. It's getting comfortable holding two opposite things at once and not requiring them to resolve.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
