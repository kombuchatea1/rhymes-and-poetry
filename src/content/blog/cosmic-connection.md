The truest love stories tend to lack good explanations. The poem is okay with that.

<div class="poem-block">

Why do you love me?<br>
"I don't know, I can't help it"<br>
How do you know it's right?<br>
"I don't know, I just feel it"

These things of intuition<br>
These little inklings<br>
Where you just know<br>
It goes beyond time

I feel like I've known you<br>
For my entire life<br>
"Maybe many moons ago—<br>
you were my wife"

This cosmic connection<br>
Of intertwined souls<br>
Makes you wonder<br>
What do we really know?

</div>

*I don't know. I just feel it.* That answer would fail an essay test. It passes the only test that matters. Some knowledge doesn't need to be argued for.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
