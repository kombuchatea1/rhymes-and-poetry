A short one. The thing I want to say to most of the people I care about, at the end of most days.

<div class="poem-block">

Crazy how we always<br>
fending for ourselves

Instead of listening and<br>
feeling unified wealth

Letting go of ego:<br>
Mental Health!

For now friend I'll say<br>
just take care of yourself

</div>

If you don't have the energy for all the rest of it today, fine. Take care of yourself. The rest of the work waits. You can pick it back up tomorrow.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
