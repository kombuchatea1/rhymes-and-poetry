Sometimes the whole point of a bar is the snap. Four lines, no preamble, the verse arriving fully formed and leaving before you've finished registering the rhyme.

This is one of those.

<div class="poem-block">

Kick 'em to the curb<br>
With our cleats!<br>
Make an impact<br>
Through rhymes & beats

</div>

In rap, there's a respect for economy that other forms don't always have. The best lines are the ones you couldn't shorten. The kick is the work — you make the impact through the cadence, the rhyme, the placement, and then you're out.

That's the goal. Not many words. Just the right ones, in the right order, hitting hard.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
