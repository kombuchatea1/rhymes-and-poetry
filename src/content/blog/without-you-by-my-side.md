A list of paradoxes. Each one is the kind of thing only love can make true.

<div class="poem-block">

Snow in spring<br>
Blues within<br>
A waltz that slows time<br>
Everything is fine

A moment engrained<br>
Photographs that fade<br>
A stranger in his town<br>
Everything is upside down

A desert with blooming trees<br>
Reality turned fantasy<br>
An ocean of souls<br>
Everything is possible with you

A clock that doesn't move<br>
A bird that doesn't fly<br>
Everything is nothing<br>
Without you by my side

</div>

The paradoxes work in both directions. The right person makes the impossible feel inevitable. The absence of them makes the inevitable feel pointless. Both are true. The poem just lays them next to each other.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
