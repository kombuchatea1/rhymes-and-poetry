A haiku. About the only honest stance toward the work of figuring out a self.

<div class="poem-block">

Mastery of Mind

Takes a Lifetime of Learning

I have just Begun

</div>

The further in you get, the more you realize how much further there is. The good news: that's the work. The whole thing.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
