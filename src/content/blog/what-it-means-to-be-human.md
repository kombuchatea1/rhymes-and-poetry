A small one about giving when you didn't really feel like it. And about what it shows you when you do.

<div class="poem-block">

I didn't want to do it<br>
I didn't think I'd care<br>
But when I saw its impact<br>
I realized what it means to share

Whether it's a simple gesture<br>
Or a little bit of your time<br>
Doing something for someone else<br>
Will help you realign

What it means to be human<br>
What it means to be kind<br>
To express oneself in unselfish ways<br>
May just be the best path to unwind

</div>

The gesture isn't always for them. Sometimes it's for the realignment it does inside you. *Doing something for someone else will help you realign.* That's the part most self-help leaves out — the helping is partly self-care, and the self-care is partly about getting out of your own head for a minute.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
