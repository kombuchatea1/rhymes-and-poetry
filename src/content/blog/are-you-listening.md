---
title: "Are You Listening?"
description: "A haiku on the quiet wisdom of the body — and the cost of ignoring it."
pubDate: 2025-07-14
category: reflections
---

Three lines. Read it once, then put your phone down for a minute and check in with your body. Then read it again.

<div class="poem-block">

Your body tells you<br>
When to act and when to rest<br>
Are you listening?

</div>

Most of the times I've been wrong about how I felt, the body knew first. Tightness in the chest before the worry surfaced. Heaviness in the legs before I admitted I was exhausted. The information was already in the building. I just hadn't picked up the message.

I'm trying to listen earlier.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
