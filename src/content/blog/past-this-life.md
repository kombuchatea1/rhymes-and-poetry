A haiku. Whatever you believe about what comes next, the love still stretches into it.

<div class="poem-block">

If Time were to stop

My Love for you would endure

Even past this Life

</div>

Some loves are bound to time. Some aren't. The poem is a small bet on the second kind.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
