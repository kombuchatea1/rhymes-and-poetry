A haiku. The honest version of religious experience, written by someone who's still figuring it out.

<div class="poem-block">

Believe in Divine

For I know not what is true

Faith is hard to keep

</div>

The not-knowing isn't the absence of faith. It's the actual condition of it. Faith without doubt isn't faith — it's just confidence. The poem is for the harder kind.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
