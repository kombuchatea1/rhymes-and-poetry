Some feelings are so heavy that the only way to carry them is to remember they're going to lift. Not because you fixed them. Just because nothing — even nightmares — gets to keep you forever.

<div class="poem-block">

Feeling the feelings<br>
We no longer wish to have

It can be like a nightmare

But just remember<br>
Nightmares are dreams

All you have to do is wake up

Just.<br>
Wake.<br>
Up!

…how long have you been dreaming?

</div>

The cruel thing about being inside a hard feeling is how permanent it feels. The kind thing about waking up is how complete the reset is — you open your eyes and the thing that was eating you ten seconds ago has no jurisdiction anymore.

The hardest part isn't getting out. It's remembering that getting out is possible.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
