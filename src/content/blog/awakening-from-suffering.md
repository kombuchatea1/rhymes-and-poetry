For anyone who's spent time in their own version of the dark. The poem doesn't pretend it was easy. Just promises it was useful.

<div class="poem-block">

Awakening from suffering<br>
Is not an easy task<br>
But lifting up your tired soul<br>
Is something that will last

If you find the strength within<br>
I know that you'll be glad<br>
For everything that happens<br>
There are lessons to be had

I wish the lessons didn't sting<br>
Quite as hard as they do<br>
But then we wouldn't appreciate<br>
All the blessings life can give to you

</div>

The trade isn't fair — you don't get to choose between the hurt and the wisdom. The wisdom only arrives via the hurt. But once it's there, the wisdom is *yours*, and it doesn't unteach itself. That's the small payment.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
