Growth isn't linear. It's circular. We come back to the same lessons in slightly different keys, and each time we're a little more able to hear them.

<div class="poem-block">

Each season has its lessons<br>
Of blessings and heartaches<br>
Awakening the soul to learn from mistakes<br>
We often doom ourselves in to partake

Again and again, in circles we flow<br>
Growth comes when we simply let go<br>
Yet we grasp on to wounds like a comfy sweater<br>
Until we realize there has always been better

All you have to do is believe<br>
It's so simple but difficult to achieve<br>
Love from within — the knitting we must weave<br>
It begins when we allow ourselves to breathe

</div>

The wounds we keep wearing aren't comfort. They're identity. The work of letting them go feels like being undressed. It's worth it.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
