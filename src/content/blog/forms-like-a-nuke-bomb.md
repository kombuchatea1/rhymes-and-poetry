Sometimes a bar is just for the bar. Internal rhyme, four references stacked, in and out.

<div class="poem-block">

I got hands like Hanz

Comparing pros and cons

Beats bump from Raekwon

My verbiage like Voltron

Cuz it forms like a nuke bomb!

</div>

The names are the wink. The cadence is the engine. The whole thing's done before you've finished the second listen.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
