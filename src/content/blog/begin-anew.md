A list. The shape of an actual quiet morning, in four words and four phrases.

<div class="poem-block">

Sunlight<br>
Beaming through my windows

Love<br>
Bundled within my blankets

Fear<br>
Feels like distant memories

Moments<br>
Time to begin anew

</div>

You don't always have to write a long poem to mark a good morning. Sometimes you just have to notice that today, the fear feels far away. Save the noticing in case the next one's harder.
