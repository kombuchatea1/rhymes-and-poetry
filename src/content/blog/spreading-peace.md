Some pieces want to be a long, slow exhale. Some want to be six bars and out. This one's the second kind.

<div class="poem-block">

I appreciate the poetry

Flowing rhapsodies for majesties

Sick of all the travesties

Too casual about the casualties

So I'm spreading peace — like a disease

I breathe — deep depth is what I hope to achieve

</div>

The world has gotten really good at being casual about casualties. That's the line that drove the rest. You can let it harden you, or you can decide to be a counterweight on the other side of the scale.

Spreading peace like a disease isn't a slogan. It's a strategy. If indifference is contagious, so is care. Pick which one you want to be a vector for.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
