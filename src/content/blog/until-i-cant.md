A small one. Holds two things at once.

<div class="poem-block">

I will keep going

Until I can't anymore

For some can't at all

</div>

The first two lines are personal. The third one widens the frame. Most days, *being able to keep going* is a privilege we don't measure. This is a small reminder to count it.

<div class="signature">
