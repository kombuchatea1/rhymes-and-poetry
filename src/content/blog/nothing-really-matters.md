Some questions don't have answers, and asking them anyway is part of being alive.

<div class="poem-block">

Nothing really matters<br>
And nothing lasts forever<br>
Yet the human spirit endeavors<br>
Through countless tales and journeys

We have our beliefs, we have our dreams<br>
Even knowing not all is what it seems<br>
We feel and feel deeply, we even fall in love<br>
Sometimes we are taken advantage of

We've always been close yet so far away<br>
The world shapes us in many different ways<br>
But remember, what we say and what we do<br>
Will always reflect upon you

We're complex, yet simple<br>
Sometimes the hard things in life<br>
Have the easiest solutions<br>
While we're here, we can be one of them

</div>

If nothing matters and we still endeavor anyway, that's not a failure of logic — that's the whole gift. We get to make meaning even when the meaning isn't built in.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
