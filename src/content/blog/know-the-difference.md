Some lessons you can only learn from the inside. This one took me too long. Sharing in case it shortens the road for someone else.

<div class="poem-block">

Do we even know what true love is?<br>
I know it isn't always easy<br>
But it doesn't make it less beautiful<br>
The hard part is knowing the difference

The difference between obsession and love<br>
The difference between drama and passion<br>
If you've been abused, your heart may see it as care<br>
I hope you grow to know the difference

Before it's too late<br>
You should know that you deserve the real thing<br>
Fight, claw, battle deep within<br>
I think you'll see the difference

Because one day you'll treat your heart<br>
The way it deserves to be treated<br>
And the people you love, or claim to love you<br>
I think you'll know the difference

</div>

If your heart has been trained to confuse intensity with care, you might need to relearn what calm love feels like. Calm doesn't mean cold. Calm means *safe.* And safe is what you actually deserve.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
