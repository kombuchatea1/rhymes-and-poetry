A haiku. The whole shape of one of the rarest feelings, in three lines.

<div class="poem-block">

Loving so deeply

As if you had met before

A sublime blessing

</div>

Some loves arrive as introductions. Some arrive as recognitions. The second kind is the one this poem is for.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
