A gratitude piece, written on a day when both the highs and the lows looked, finally, like they had been on the same team the whole time.

<div class="poem-block">

Grateful for the highs

Grateful for the lows

Happy that I've grown

And gone through the flows,

Phases, and paths

That have shaped me

To be here now, it goes fast

No longer dwelling on the past

And next time I fall

It won't be so hard

I've got myself to lean on

</div>

The most useful person to learn how to lean on is the one who never has to leave the room.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
