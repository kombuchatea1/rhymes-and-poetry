A small one. The kind of question that visits late at night.

<div class="poem-block">

I wonder too

What life could have been

If I loved myself sooner

And nurtured what's within

</div>

You can wonder. You can even grieve a little for the version that didn't happen. But you can't go back and do it. The only place self-love is still available is the one you're standing in. Start there.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
