Another small one in two languages. Different shape, same root.

<div class="poem-block">

Meu coração é<br>
Amor e alma sempre<br>
Eu ainda choro

—

My heart is<br>
Love and soul always<br>
I still cry

</div>

Crying isn't a failure. The capacity to still cry, after everything, is the same capacity that lets you still feel the rest of it. Don't lose either.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
