A small one. Boiled down to the simplest possible vocabulary.

<div class="poem-block">

This is real, I'm not gonna conceal

This is real, and I just wanna feel

A part of something I can be proud of

and Love; Respect, Connect

Respect. Connect.

</div>

The closing repetition is the whole point. *Respect. Connect.* Most of what we're searching for collapses, eventually, into those two words.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
