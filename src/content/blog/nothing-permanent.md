A haiku. Possibly the shortest version of the longest lesson.

<div class="poem-block">

When you discover

There is nothing permanent

Then you will be free

</div>

The grip is what hurts. Not the change. The change is going to happen either way. The freedom is in the loosening of the grip.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
