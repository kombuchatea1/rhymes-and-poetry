A small one. About the strange fact that the *you* you see is never quite the same one twice.

<div class="poem-block">

When I look in the mirror

What do I see?

If you asked me

I'd say I see someone new

Every day

Sometimes I like him

Sometimes I don't

</div>

That's the honest report. The you in the mirror is shifting all the time, and the relationship to him is too. Both versions — the *like* and the *don't* — are temporary. So is the next one.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
