For the love that surprises you with its depth — and for the small, repeated work of staying near it.

<div class="poem-block">

Don't cry my love<br>
Because if you do<br>
I'll cry too

For your pain is my pain<br>
And your joy is my happiness<br>
I never knew this feeling could exist

Two souls and many goals<br>
Two wholes acting as halves<br>
In support and growth to be had

A love so pure — it feels like a dream<br>
It seems otherworldly<br>
This plain of peace

So when I see you sad<br>
I promise I'll be there (always)<br>
And wipe away your tears

</div>

*Two wholes acting as halves.* That's the whole secret. Not two halves becoming a whole — two whole people choosing, daily, to act as part of something larger. The dependence is a choice, not a wound.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
