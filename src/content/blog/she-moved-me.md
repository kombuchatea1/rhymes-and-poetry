Some people leave the room and stay in the room at the same time. This is for one of them.

<div class="poem-block">

You remind me<br>
Of a girl I used to know

She had so much soul<br>
She loved to write poems<br>
She'd roam on her own

An inner fire<br>
That Seduced me<br>
And Induced me<br>
To be a better man

To have a better plan<br>
For this journey called life<br>
She's gone now<br>
But like a movie scene:<br>
She moved me

</div>

The most lasting love letters aren't always to the people we end up with. Sometimes they're to the ones who briefly rearranged the architecture and then left. *She moved me.* That stays.

<div class="signature">

— JTC
