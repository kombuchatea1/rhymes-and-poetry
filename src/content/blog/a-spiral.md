Some loves are gentle. Some are spirals. This one's about the second kind.

<div class="poem-block">

Loving you is easy<br>
Loving you is sad

Loving you with hatred<br>
I guess that's kinda bad.

I'm feeling like<br>
I'm feeling like<br>
I'm feeling like<br>
A Spiral.

Pain enough to set me off the edge

It's a cycle

</div>

The repeating *I'm feeling like* is the spiral itself doing its work — the same thought looping until it becomes the only thought. The only way out is naming it as a cycle, then deciding to step off.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
