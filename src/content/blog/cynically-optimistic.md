Two seemingly contradictory traits, sharing one body. This is for the version of me that holds both at once.

<div class="poem-block">

Cynically optimistic<br>
Forgive but never forget<br>
Let go of all that hurt inside<br>
Or live with much regret

Bow to the chaos of the universe<br>
For you never had a choice<br>
Pain and happiness are temporary<br>
So listen to your voice

For you know deep down<br>
What is pure and what is not<br>
What is meant for your soul<br>
And what must be forgot

Even if it hurts…<br>
Know there'll be brighter days<br>
And when they come, cherish them<br>
Appreciating always

</div>

The voice underneath is usually right. The trick is just being quiet enough to hear it.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
