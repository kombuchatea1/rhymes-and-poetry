Another running poem. Different angle. The one about what running is *for*, not just what it does.

<div class="poem-block">

Running is my self-reflection<br>
A dance of willpower<br>
A daily meditation<br>
An end I always celebrate

To put one foot in front of the other<br>
Not knowing where you'll go<br>
Is courage we seldom cherish<br>
Even better if you take it slow

I tend to run the same path<br>
Sometimes it's a chore<br>
Yet I often find things<br>
I had never seen before

In ways, it mirrors life itself<br>
The sacrifice, the grit, the stakes<br>
And one day we won't be able to<br>
So why not run before it's too late?

</div>

The running won't be available forever. The body has a window. *So why not run before it's too late?* — same logic for almost anything you've been meaning to do.

— JTC
