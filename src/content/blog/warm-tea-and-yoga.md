Some haikus are observations. This one is a recipe.

<div class="poem-block">

Spiritual breath

Depths of soul begin to calm

Warm tea and yoga

</div>

The cure for most low-grade dread isn't elaborate. It's usually some combination of *stop*, *breathe*, *warm*, *move*. The trick is just remembering that the simple version of the answer is also the real one.

<div class="signature">

— JTC

</div>
