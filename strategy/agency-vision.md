# Agency vision (Jake + wife)

## The bigger plan

Rhymes & Poetry is the personal brand. The longer-term goal is an AI agency that Jake is building with his wife, with R&P serving as the first proof-of-work case study. Every script, workflow, migration playbook, and pattern we build for R&P should be considered potential agency IP.

## Roles

- **Jake:** biz dev, sales, client relationships. Solutions Engineer at Push Security — credible at translating tech to business value. Learning web dev through this build to also apply at Push.
- **Jake's wife:** technical build, code, AI workflow construction. Already a coder, learning AI specifically.
- **Claude (this agent):** build partner, documentation, learning amplifier. Narrate the *why* of decisions, not just the *what*.

## Likely niche directions (not yet picked)

Most credible given what we've built:

**"Brand revival for small businesses stuck on Wix / Squarespace / Weebly"** — migrate them to a modern stack (Astro + Cloudflare or similar), set up AI-powered content workflows, hand back a faster, cheaper, more flexible site. The R&P rebuild itself is the case study for this offering.

Other directions worth thinking about:

- Content automation for creator brands
- AI back-office for service businesses under $5M revenue
- AI implementation for niche professional services (law, accounting, healthcare practices)

The Wix-revival angle is the most directly supportable by what we've already shipped. Worth picking that as the lead offering and adding others as second-tier menu items.

## What to document as we build

For every meaningful script or workflow added to R&P:

- A short README explaining what it does
- The decision context (why this approach vs. alternatives)
- A "client-ready" version that strips out R&P-specific paths and could be deployed elsewhere

This converts our R&P work into a portable agency toolkit over time.

## Position statement (draft — not final)

> We help small creator brands and service businesses migrate off rigid hosting platforms onto modern AI-powered stacks. Our work is documented end-to-end through our own brand build — you can see what we ship before you hire us.

Needs refinement once we have more case-study artifacts. Revisit after R&P has 6 months of live data.

## What's missing before going public with an agency offering

- A landing page for the agency (separate from R&P, or a subdomain)
- 2–3 documented "playbook" patterns (Wix migration, content pipeline, etc.) packaged as client-ready offerings
- Pricing (rough: $5–15k for a Wix migration; $1–3k/mo for ongoing content pipeline management)
- A clear answer to "why you and not [competitor X]" — currently best answer is "because you can see exactly what we built for ourselves"
