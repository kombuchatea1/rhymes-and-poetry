# Brand positioning

## Brand promise

Honest poetry and reflection. The line that doesn't leave you.

## What R&P is

A personal poetry brand by Jake Cohn (JTC). The site holds ~146 poems and counting — most are reflective, some are love and loss, some are short rap-leaning verses, all written in Jake's voice. The site exists because Instagram is a feed that buries work; R&P is the long memory.

## What R&P is NOT

- A content mill or SEO play
- A poetry "utility platform" — no rhyme finders, no poem generators, no caption generators
- A brand competing on volume or AI-generated work
- A platform with premium tiers or aggressive monetization

## Voice principles

**Use:**

- Plain words. "I" and "you" liberally. Short sentences when they land.
- Specific imagery. *"A room of plants with not enough water"* beats *"neglected priorities."*
- Honest emotional admission. *"I'm scared / That one day / I might stop"* — the poems don't perform strength.
- The `— JTC` signature as the closer.
- Conversational asides ("kinda cool," "thanks for reading," "that'd be cool").

**Avoid:**

- Buzzwords: unlock, unleash, elevate, tapestry, embark, realm, journey (as a verb)
- Generic motivational language
- "Content" to refer to poems — use "poems," "pieces," or "writing"
- Hype, excessive exclamation points, "Don't miss this!"
- AI tells: delve, leveraging, tapestry, in conclusion, it's important to note

## The voice in one paragraph

R&P sounds like a person who has been writing quietly for years, talking honestly about what he's noticed. Not a brand. Not a wellness influencer. Not an AI. The poems are the work; the prose intros and outros around them are the small human moments of context that make a poem feel placed in someone's life.
